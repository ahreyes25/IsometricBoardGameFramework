Ground:
	000 = grass
	001 = dirt
	002 = water
	
Decoration:
	000 = no decoration
	010 = building
	020 = tree
	030 = mountain
	
Units:
	000 = no unit
	100 = team1
	200 = team2
	
States:
	1000 = waiting
	2000 = idle
	3000 = ...
	etc
	
Final Examples:
	Space with Dirt and Building and no unit				= 0011
	Space with water and unit on team 1 that is waiting		= 1102
	Space with water and unit on team 1 that is idle		= 2102
	Space with grass and mountain and no unit				= 030
